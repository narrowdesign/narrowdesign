Travolta combines the pleasure of flying with the pleasure of giving. Every Christmas, he and Preston invite family members to their house in Maine, and both the Gulfstream and the 707 make pickups from Hawaii eastward. When Barbra Streisand and James Brolin married in Malibu, the Gulfstream was theirs for a honeymoon trip to the Caribbean. "John personally chose everything that he knew Barbra liked" says Preston. "He made sure the plane was just right for them."
