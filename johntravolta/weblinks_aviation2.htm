Bombardier Learjet
Contains all you ever wanted to know about private jets - Lears, Continentals, Challengers, Canadair SE's and more. Gives technical data, flying costs, specs, a listing of products and retail services, facilities, a media center and gallery, history and job search function.



Aircraft Owners and Pilots Association
The official Website of the AOPA has everything serious fliers need, from up-to-date news affecting airways and air safety to runway diagrams to the nation's 330 busiest ports, plus essays, observations and notices by pilots around the world.

Aviation Now
McGraw-Hills' famed Aviation Week magazine posts breaking news and in-depth pieces on aviation around the world, including space, military, business, air show and general aviation.

Boeing Aviation
The official site of the Seattle-based aerospace giant contains not only job, stock and company news but fascinating details on the aircraft manufacturer's newest planes, including the Rotocraft and the Minuteman III missle.

Lockheed Martin
The aeronautical company's official site posts news on all its aircraft, from the behemoth C-140 to the ferocious Atlas rocket. There's also a dramatic photo and video gallery - a plane-lover's paradise - documenting some of the company's historical launches.
