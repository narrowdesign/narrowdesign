Swordfish
On Deck…John is teaming up with Mega-producer, Joel Silver (Lethal Weapon Series, Die Hard Trilogy, and The Matrix Series) for the action thriller - Swordfish. John will star as the world's most dangerous spy who is hired by the CIA to coerce a computer hacker (X-Men's Hugh Jackman) to help steal six billion dollars in government funds. Fish is slated to be released upon June 15th, 2001 and figures to be a force to be reckoned with during next summer's action movie season.

Domestic Disturbance
On The Lot…John will soon go into production on the psychological thriller, Domestic Disturbance, in which John plays a divorced father who discovers that his 11-year-old son's new stepfather is not what he made himself out to be. Fearing for the safety of his little boy, John's character sets out on a mission to rescue him from danger, and in the process goes head-to-head with the amiable psychopath who threatens all of their lives.
