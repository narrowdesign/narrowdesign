There's a common thread stitching all this together.

It runs through each of us.

It weaves its way through every important thing we ever created.

If you look hard enough, you'll see it

And no matter how much technology we invent

It'll keep being what stops us all from falling to pieces.

And when some passionate, creative, thoughtful human.

has some wild idea she can't shake out of her head,

An untold story that needs a voice.

Some beloved character who won't go out on anybody else's terms.

It'll be this thread that helps it live, or dance, or sing, or shine, or fly.

And when the creative obsession is MINE,

When I'M the crazy one,

When I need it out of MY skull and into the world,

Who'll champion my idea?

Who'll encourage me? challenge me? validate me?

Be entertained and celebrate with me?

Without them, even my most powerful ideas will die.

So when I'm ready,

Ready to make real that thing I hope to be remembered for.

Will you support me?
